# Product Guidelines

## Communication Tone and Style
The project's documentation and communication will adopt a tone that is **Clear and Concise**, ensuring information is presented directly and efficiently. It will also be **Engaging and Accessible**, using language and approaches that make complex topics understandable to a broad interdisciplinary audience without sacrificing accuracy.

## Technical Explanation Detail
Technical explanations within the project's documentation will feature **High-level overviews** to provide quick understanding of concepts and functionality. Additionally, they will include **Practical examples and tutorials** with step-by-step guides and code snippets to demonstrate usage and common tasks, facilitating practical application.

## Interdisciplinary Approach
Given the project's interdisciplinary nature, the documentation will **Integrate and explain cross-disciplinary concepts**. This means seamlessly weaving together concepts from both theoretical computer science and biology, providing explanations for terms that might be new to one audience but familiar to the other, fostering a deeper, holistic understanding.

## Visual Identity and Branding
The visual identity and branding for `computingMicrobiome` will be **Scientific and minimalist**. This approach emphasizes a clean, professional design, prioritizing data clarity and precise presentation of scientific information with minimal embellishments in all documentation, visualizations, and overall project presentation.
